A portable charger has become as essential as a phone case, especially when you’re juggling a drained battery between meetings, flights, or a long day out. The key is finding one that balances capacity, size, and speed without eating into your budget. Most budget-friendly options land between 10,000mAh and 20,000mAh, enough to top off a phone one to four times, and they usually rely on standard USB-A or USB-C ports. When choosing, look at what devices you’ll charge: phones with fast charging need a power bank that supports the same protocol, and multi-device users want at least two ports. Build quality and brand reputation matter more than a tiny weight difference, as does the ability to recharge the power bank itself quickly. The three picks below cover the spectrum from bare-bones value to a do-it-all performer, all without demanding a splurge.
